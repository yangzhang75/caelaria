Conversion Flow

A simple working prototype from interest to follow-up.

Visitors can land on a service page, evaluate ready-made or custom options, submit a unified intake form, and trigger a follow-up workflow that packages their request for internal response.

1. Browse

Start from Home, Escapes, Labs, Consulting, or Orders to understand the available service lines.

2. Choose

Select a ready-made module or custom project path based on audience, use case, and readiness goals.

3. Submit

Use one intake form to share organization details, service line, scope, budget, timing, and compliance context.

4. Follow Up

The prototype routes the request into a prepared email workflow and sends visitors to a confirmation page.

Proof of Direction

Why this prototype is built for conversion.

  • Each business line has its own landing page with examples, value, and a clear CTA.
  • The Orders hub supports both ready-made purchases and custom build requests.
  • The intake form captures enough detail to route a project without overwhelming the visitor.
